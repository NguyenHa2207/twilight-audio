Product Details

14.8mm Planar Magnetic Driver
Shuoer has developed a custom 14.8mm large planar magnetic driver for the latest S12 in-ear monitors. It enables the pair to produce exceptional sound performance with powerful lower end, crisp midrange, and detailed treble frequencies. Perfect sound for electronics pops and other genres.It’s a showstopper for your music life.

Minimalist Industrial Design
Shuoer S12 ear cavities are crafted using a high-precision CNC machining process. They are made up of high-quality aluminum alloy material polished in a smooth and elegant matte finish.

High-Purity Silver-Plated Cable
Shuoer S12 comes packed with a high-purity silver-plated monocrystalline copper cable. The sound signal faces low internal resistance from the cable, ensuring crisp sound with lower distortion. This cable has 2-pin 0.78mm connectors.

Ergonomics Design
Designed based on large data of human ear canals, Shuoer S12 is born to be a comfortable in ear monitors even for longtime wearing. Secure fit and exquisite faceplate complements each other. Simply bring this product and enjoy your HiFi world on the go.
